Limitations

The language model is highly intelligent but was trained on data up to September 2021. Therefore it cannot:

  • Know about knowledge from last couple years.
  • Answer questions about current events like weather and politics.

Additionally:

  • For languages other that English and Arabic, you may receive server errors (500) during peak times. We're working on increasing our capacity, and building funds, to provide a better service.
  • Answers may be convincing but not truthful.
  • The AI is trained to refuse inappropriate requests. Any actions you take as a result of the AI are yours alone.

We hope to bring improvements for many of these aspects soon!
