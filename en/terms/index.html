Data we collect

Limitations of the Service

Due to our use of OpenAI's models, we inherit their limitations. When asking linguaGPT questions:

  • The system may occasionally generate incorrect or misleading information and produce offensive or biased content.

However, there are safeguards in place to ensure the model generates inoffensive responses.
